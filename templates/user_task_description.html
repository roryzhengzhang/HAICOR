Your Task Description

What you are invited to do?

Our AI is far from 'perfect', it may have chance to predict human needs with an inappropriate path, which causes the prediction going awry. your job is to identify the inadequacy of reasoning paths it employs and rectify the place where you don't agree with. To modify the graph, you are allowed to add, delete, alter the concepts (node) and relations (edge) freely, the criteria is that you need to inject your own knowledge into the path modification as much as possible. As you are satisfied with your revision of the paths, you are invited to select the top-K paths that you believe will lead the AI to find the right human needs, based on which our AI will re-predict and display the updated humans needs to you. You will then be asked to evaluate the quality of the updated answer.
