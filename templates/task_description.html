Task Description

AI's task

We have an smart AI whose job is to read a simple story and speculate the basic human needs behind the character's behavior. However, most of these human needs are not spoken out in the story, the AI has to reason about the answer based on its commonsense knowledge. AI's knowledge can be represented as a connected graph, where each node represents a specific concept (existent: book, car, shop, etc; non-existent: morning, happy, eat, etc) and edge correpsonds to the relation between two concepts (comedy 'causes' happy). This knowledge graph enables AI to 'think deeper' than just stay on the literal description of story.

How AI makes reasoning?

First, let's think of how our human makes reasoning. General speaking, our human maintains enormous logic rules in our brain, for each rule A --c--> B, it tells us B can be achieved given A based on the relation C. For example, the rule ‘exam --- causes ---> study’ means that if we have ‘exam’ in our brain, the concept ‘study’ will be perceived since it can be obtained through this rule. In our case, the proper human needs are those reachable along the chain of rules starting from the keywords in the story.

Our AI imitates the human reasoning strategy. Briefly, the AI will read a pair of story and question and maintain a knowledge graph (KG) at the same time, it will then reason about the human needs based on the keywords in the context and question over the KG.
