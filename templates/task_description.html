AI's task

We have an smart AI whose job is to read a simple story and speculate the basic human needs behind the character's behavior. However, most of these human needs are not spoken out in the story, the AI has to reason about the answer based on its commonsense knowledge. AI's knowledge can be represented as a connected graph like below, where each node represents a specific concept (existent: book, car, shop, etc; non-existent: morning, happy, eat, etc) and edge correpsonds to the relation between two concepts (comedy 'causes' happy). This knowledge graph enables AI to 'think deeper' than just stay on the literal description of story.
