Explanation of Terminology

What is commonsense knowledge?

All the knowledge we take as granted can be seen as commonsense knowledge. Formally, the commonsense is defined as ' implications shared by the competent users of a language'. The first type of coomonsense knoledge is from human's good sense: 'the knacks for seeing things as they are and doing thing as they ought to be done'; Besides, commonsense can also be folk wisdom: "signifying unreflective knowledge not reliant on specialized training or deliberative thought".

How does the knowledge graph build up?

Computer scientists collect the relation among various concepts from the following main venues:

  • Wikipedia
  • English dictionary
  • Crowdsoucring

For example, The relation 'Band of Brother --castActor--> Neal McDonough' is drawn from Wikipedia item 'Band of Brother' whose casting includes 'Neal McDonough as First Lieutenant Lynn "Buck" Compton', and from item 'Neal McDonough' we can know his mother language is English
