How AI predict human needs based on story and commonsense graph?

To be specific, the AI will find paths connecting ‘human need’ and keywords, those human needs arrivable from keywords are viewed as candidate. For example, for the sentence “He came home with a gold medal”, the AI will extract the keyword like ‘gold’, ‘medal’ and ‘god medal’, according to the subgraph, the human needs ‘status’ will be a candidate thanks to the paths among it and keywords. Then the AI will score each path with a standard metric, and predict the most likely human need according to the top-ranked paths and story context.
